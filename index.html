FIST-D
Finger See Things Differently

Explosive ordnance disposal is among the most hazardous occupations. We mitigate the risks by developing a EOD robot that can be teleoperated from afar. Currently available robots depend heavily on vision and video telemetry, which can be drastically impaired when IEDs are concealed. This research addresses this scientific challenge by developing a robotic system with multi-modal perceptual image comprised of tactile, optical, and force information.
